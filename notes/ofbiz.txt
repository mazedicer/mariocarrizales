//DIRECTORY STRUCTURE
///////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////////
/*
framework/
    components that run ofbiz
    components depend on each other
    loaded first

application/
    business related components
    management of content, orders, etc

specialpurpose/
    more components
    
themes/
    facades
    
hot-deploy/
    for add-on components and apps
*/
